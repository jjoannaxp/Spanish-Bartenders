Let's learn Spanish!

¡Hola! Welcome to The Spanish Bartender, a video game designed to teach you Spanish while running your own virtual bar. Our game utilizes gamification techniques to make learning fun and engaging for English-speaking individuals between the ages of 10 and 50. To get started, create an account and log in. You'll be the owner of a virtual bar in a Spanish-speaking country. You'll need to manage your bar, serve customers, and converse with them in Spanish. As you progress through the levels, the difficulty will increase, and you'll encounter new vocabulary and grammar.

Features



  • Improve your Spanish vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation skills
  • Real-time feedback on pronunciation and grammar
  • Manage your own virtual bar
  • Track your progress and compare with other players
  • Earn in-game currency to upgrade your bar and unlock new challenges


Levels





Basic Level
Introduction to grammar and vocabulary

- Introduction to basic grammar rules

- Building vocabulary

- Practice with simple sentences and basic conversation

Focus: Conjugation

Intermediate Level
More complex grammar and vocabulary

- Review of basic grammar and vocabulary

- Practice with more complex sentence structures and conversation

- Introduction to idiomatic expressions and advanced vocabulary

Focus: Speaking and listening skills

Advanced Level
Mastery of grammar, vocabulary, and conversation skills

- Mastery of grammar, advanced vocabulary, and conversation skills

- Reading and writing practice at an advanced level

- Focus on fluency and expression

Focus: Cultural awareness and immersion
