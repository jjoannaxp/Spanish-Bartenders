Meet the Team!

The team at The Spanish Bartender is commited to helping people learn spanish in a fun and engaging way!

Our team is made up of 6 inspiring innovators: Dani, Joey, Shailee, Zac, Louis, and Yeji.


The mission of our company is to game-ify learning Spanish. We know that learning a new language can be stressful, boring, and difficult, so we are here to make it better! Weither you are a beginner, intermediate, or advanced, The Spanish Bartender is a game that id fun for all ages and skill levels!
