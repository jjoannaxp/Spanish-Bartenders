ABOUT THE GAME
Story
You quit your job as a business analyst, move to Mexico impulsively after finding out that your late grandfather has a bar there in your name. You soon find a companion in Professor Aguila who helps you as you endeavor running a bar for Spanish-speaking locals!
Characters
Professor Aguila
A former language professor for a school that got shut down, Professor Aguila is a master at language despite his lack of a job. He is your main companion and teaches you how to speak with your customers in their native tongue!
You
Previously a business analyst, you realize that your late grandfather's old bar has been handed down to you. Dealing with the pressure of living up to his past expectations, you take on the mission of learning how to run a bar with little experience while also learning a new language.
