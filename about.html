About Our Company

Overview

The Spanish Bartender aims to provide an interactive and engaging platform for individuals to learn the Spanish language. By using gamification techniques, users will be able to improve their language skills while having fun. The target audience is primarily English-speaking individuals between the ages of 10 and 50. The platform will offer various levels of difficulty, allowing users to progress as they improve their language skills. The company plans to generate revenue through a monthly subscription model and through the sale of advertisements.
